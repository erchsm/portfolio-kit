Micro App Templates

A quick introduction to the Wrap platform — a Wrap is a highly-focused, app-like, mobile web experience.

Wraps are mobile web apps and live at a URL. The flexibility of the URL allows a end user to enter the Wrap through many channels including social feeds, e-mail, web advertisement or, like shown below, through SMS.

These experiences are made up cards — a new "page" of the traditional "app" is analagous to a card. The creation and distribution of the experiences was handled through a SASS application that we developed in-house as well.

the creation of The way that myself and the design team designed wraps was by designing cards. Wrap Components are the building blocks of those cards. They can be positioned at will on the card.

I designed and developed mobile-first web interactions for our product and external clients.
